🔴 The Public Investment Fund Enhances Global Investors' Access to the Saudi Market 🇸🇦👇

The Public Investment Fund announced the launch of a Saudi equities ETF in collaboration with State Street Investment Management, serving as a lead investor in the new fund.

🔹 The fund (SAQL) enables investors in Europe to access opportunities in the Saudi market
🔹 It relies on a multi-factor active investment model to select stocks
🔹 Listed in Germany and also listed in London
🔹 It reflects the fund's direction to enhance the Saudi market's global attractiveness
🔹 This step confirms the continued inflow of foreign investments and the development of innovative financial instruments in the Saudi market
